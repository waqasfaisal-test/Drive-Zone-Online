Update 1.4.1 – What’s new

Stability fixes, smoother play, and a new career lobby.

The new update improves stability and adds useful changes. It fixes crashes and freezes, improves performance, and makes the game run more smoothly. A new career lobby is also added to expand the experience.

Full changes

  • Crashes in the open world during tuning changes are fixed.
  • Freezes at 100% loading are removed.
  • Performance is better when headlights are on.
  • Horizon draw distance works as expected.
  • A new career lobby is added.
  • Collision issues on open world maps are fixed.
  • Tuning bugs on several cars are resolved.
  • Car lights no longer disappear in some map areas.
  • Time of day stays in sync when joining a room by ID.
